  value_equation:
    level: "Originator of the framework"
    specific_knowledge:
      - "Dream Outcome: what does the customer want to achieve? Be specific. Not 'better compliance' but 'zero FDA audit failures'"
      - "Perceived Likelihood of Achievement: does the customer believe this will work for them? Social proof, case studies, credentials matter here."
      - "Time Delay: how long until they see results? Shorter is more valuable. 'Results in 30 days' beats 'gradual improvement.'"
      - "Effort and Sacrifice: how much work does the customer have to do? Less is more valuable. The done-for-you offer is always worth more than done-by-you."
      - "The denominator is underestimated: halving time delay or effort doubles perceived value as much as doubling the dream outcome"
    translation_to_stillwater:
      - "Dream Outcome for regulated industry buyers: 'Pass the FDA audit with full AI agent history'"
      - "Perceived Likelihood: CRIO's 70% audit risk reduction is the credibility anchor — Phuc has done this before"
      - "Time Delay: 'Deploy in one afternoon with launch-swarm.sh' — minimal time to first value"
      - "Effort: 'Copy-paste the prompt. The swarm does the work.' — minimal effort barrier"

    specific_knowledge:
